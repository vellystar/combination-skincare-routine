Know Your Skin

Combination Skin

✦ What is Combination Skin?

Combination skin has an oily T-zone (forehead, nose, chin) and normal-to-dry cheeks. It needs a balanced approach — controlling oil without stripping moisture.

  • T-zone is oily, shiny, prone to clogged pores & breakouts
  • Cheeks can feel dry, tight, or normal
  • Pores more visible on nose and chin
  • Can experience breakouts and dry patches simultaneously
  • Skin needs change with seasons, hormones and stress
💧

Hydration is Key

Even oily skin needs moisture. Skipping moisturiser triggers excess sebum production as skin tries to compensate.

🔬

Low pH Cleansers

Always use pH 4.5–5.5 cleansers to preserve your skin's acid mantle and healthy microbiome.

☁️

Lightweight Formulas

Gel moisturisers hydrate without overwhelming oilier zones while nourishing drier areas.

🛡️

Barrier Repair

Ceramides and centella strengthen the barrier, regulate sebum, reduce sensitivity and even texture.

☀️

SPF Every Day

UV exposure worsens oiliness, dryness and uneven texture. Daily sunscreen keeps results lasting.

✦

Ingredients to Love

Centella asiatica, snail mucin, hyaluronic acid, ceramides, niacinamide — calming and hydrating without clogging.

⚠️ Avoid These

  • Harsh foaming cleansers with high pH
  • Heavy occlusive oils on the T-zone
  • Over-exfoliating (max 1–2x per week)
  • Alcohol-based toners
  • Skipping moisturiser even on oily days
